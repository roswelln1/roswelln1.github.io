Shrek Tek is a company started in 2016 by Drew Smith and Katie Klein dedicated to making the newest technological advancements available to everyone.

While studying physics and astrology together at the University of Dayton, Drew and Katie began researching solar energy and made a discovery that would change not only their lives, but the lives of potentially millions. For years scientists have harnessed and utilized the suns solar rays, but no one ever imagined the moon’s lunar rays could be even more powerful. Drew and Katie found a way to capture these lunar rays and discovered that when the rays are applied to food, the calories vanish!

Technology this advanced could be sold for billions, but Drew and Katie interests lied elsewhere. Rather than reserving the valuable technology to a select few, Katie and Drew founded “Shrek Tek”; a technology company that prides itself on providing the latest and greatest innovations in the science community to customers at an affordable price.

Shrek Tek’s first product, EFF-it, is a fast acting calorie eliminator that has taken the world by storm. Our target market, college students, love the product because of its effectiveness and affordability. Shrek Tek believes that college students are the minds and leaders of the future. Co-founders Drew and Katie know from experience how difficult college life can be, specifically in terms of maintaining personal health and financial budgeting. The EFF-it allows users to consume whatever they desire without the repercussions, gaining weight.

Shrek Tek believes the EFF-it will make our company a household name and pave the way for future product lines. But this is just the beginning. Here at Shrek Tek our customers are more then just sales, to us they are the future. Our employees and scientists share this belief and are dedicated to discovering innovative technology and providing it at an affordable price.
