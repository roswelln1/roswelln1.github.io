Industry Analysis: Trends in fitness industry

According to Wellness Creative Company the fitness industry is rapidly growing. In revenue terms, the fitness industry growth rate is 2.6% globally. According to the latest International, Health Racquet and Sportsclub Association (IHRSA) report, the total fitness industry revenue was an estimated $83.1bn in 2016, up from $81bn in 2015. The industry revenue totaled $87.2 billion in 2017, supporting an even bigger increase. If it continues on a similar trajectory then the industry should reach around $90bn in 2018. It’s also worth noting that some markets outperformed others, with the US increasing buy 7.2% (www.ihrsa.org). A significant trend in the current fitness industry is the new wave of the digital trend (www.wellnesscreatives.com). Technology is driving the fitness industry and many companies are taking advantage of this trend especially younger markets.
